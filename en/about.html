ABOUT US

SEOPUNG, A Company Built on Trust

A seafood company with 50 years of tradition - a story of quality and trust spanning two generations

"Success is a direction,
and happiness is the journey"

SEOPUNG Fishery Cooperative Corporation is a seafood processing company located in Hwayangmyeon, Yeosu-si, Jeollanam-do. Situated in an optimal location adjacent to the pristine waters of the South Sea, we produce high-quality processed seafood based on the cooperation of fishing communities.

As of 2023, we achieved revenue of 35.6 billion KRW and operating profit of 2.2 billion KRW, growing our revenue by approximately 7 times over 10 years. We supply seafood to numerous educational institutions including Yeosu Yangji Elementary School and Ansim Elementary School.
